Privacy Policy

DriveMaster Theory 2026
Effective Date: February 15, 2026

1. Data Storage

DriveMaster is architected with a "Local-First" philosophy. All your learning progress, quiz history, mock test results, and readiness scores are stored strictly locally on your device using secure internal storage. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your personal learning data to any external cloud server.

2. Critical: Data Retention

Because your data lives 100% on your device, uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage via Android Settings will result in the permanent loss of your progress. We cannot recover lost data because we never had access to it in the first place.

3. Premium Features

If you unlock Premium features, that status is stored securely on your device. Just like your progress, this unlock is tied to your current installation on this specific device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses standard Android/iOS APIs for functionality (such as Notifications for study reminders and URL Launcher for opening links). No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs are active in the current release.
